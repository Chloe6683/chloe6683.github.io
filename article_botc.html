Mist & Madness

Mist & Madness is the showcase script for the Urchin & the Tombcrawler. The Tombcrawler provides cover for the Lleech, while the Cerenovus, Mutant, and Urchin cast doubt on claims.

The Blame Game


The Blame Game is the showcase script for the Warden. It focuses around execution survival as confirmation, with the Vortox obscuring the information-based roles.
