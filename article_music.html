I'm Listening To:

This is a collection of music I'm listening to, or was last time I remembered to update my site. I'd recommend it all :)

BE N!CE

"BE N!CE are a London-based band colliding harsh flavours of punk with post-punk, hardcore, grunge & alternative metal influences."

BE N!CE are possibly my favourite band at the minute, and were amazing live.

I'd recommend ONE BAD APPLE and GET A JOB the most.

Moth Slut

"Notorious for dramatic visuals and intense live shows, the duo rose through the DIY space and made a name for themselves in the wider UK goth scene with a delicious blend of 80's drum machines and modern synth sensibilities. "

Most Slut were the band that got me into Goth music, and have possibly the best stage presence of any band I've seen.

I'd recommend Times Like This and Omni Tempore the most.
